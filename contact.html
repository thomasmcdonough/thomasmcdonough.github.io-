It all started a a backyard barbecue. Our grill-master had had enough! "No more requests, ya think you're special" Hoova shouted! "Why not, I am special, why shouldn't my burger be" Lauren replied. My dear friend Hoova looked at me muttered "I should be getting paid for this". Boom! An idea was born. Four people, four personalities, four very different burgers, that described us so perfectly we just had to share our burger manifested selves with you! We invite you to stop in explore your own foodie personality and share a little abut you with us! We look forward to meeting... Everybody!
